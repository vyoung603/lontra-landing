Why Lontra

Lontra is the genus name for otters.

Otters are resilient, social, and endlessly adaptable. They float on their backs, hold hands to stay connected as they ride the waves, and navigate turbulent waters with something that looks a lot like ease. We're building Lontra to bring that same vibe to your mind: clarity, support, and an ever-present partner as you move through the world.
